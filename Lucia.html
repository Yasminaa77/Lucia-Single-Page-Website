Who are we?

The Lucia is made up of a group of passionate people who started this local business in Vancouver. We are looking forward to creating an extraordinary experience while enjoying your drinks. All extracts are 100% organic and taken from the local Canadian fields. Extracts can be used in your morning coffee or evening teas and even in desserts! You can also check out our blog to get more than only the products.
